Good to Know

Frequently asked questions.

Do you handle both design and manufacturing?

Yes. We are a design-and-build atelier with our own workshop in Al Quoz — from concept and technical drawings to in-house fabrication and on-site installation.

Which areas do you serve?

We deliver projects across Dubai and the wider UAE, for residential, commercial, and hospitality clients.

Can you work from an architect's or designer's drawings?

Absolutely. We collaborate with architects, designers, and contractors, or we can develop the full design with you from scratch.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines depend on scope, but most bespoke joinery projects run from a few weeks to a few months. We confirm a clear schedule before work begins.

What materials and standards do you use?

We work with European hardwoods, natural stone, and premium hardware, finished to a millimetre tolerance for a flawless, lasting result.
